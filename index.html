Biography

I am a Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. HENG Pheng-Ann and Prof. WONG, Tien-Tsin. Previously, I received my B.Sc. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2018.

I am currently interested in deep learning-based automatic chest radiograph analysis, including efficient learning, robust learning, and interesting findings on clinical applications.
