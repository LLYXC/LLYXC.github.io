Biography

I am currently a post-doctoral fellow in the Department of Biomedical Informatics, Harvard University, working with Prof. Pranav Rajpurkar. Before that, I have been worked as a post-doctoral fellow with Prof. Hao Chen at HKUST. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the CSE department, CUHK, advised by Prof. HENG Pheng-Ann and Prof. WONG, Tien-Tsin. Previously, I obtained my B.Sc. degree from the CSE department, CUHK.

My research interest majorly focuses on developing efficient and trustworthy deep learning algorithms for medical data analysis. Currently, I am particularly interested in developing and applying foundation models for multimodal biomedical data analysis.
